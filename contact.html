frequently asked questions

Q:How long does an order take to be delivered?

A: Our delivery times depend on the location. Typically, an order anywhere in the U.S. can be fulfilled in 3-4 weeks.

Q:Are there wholesale discounts?

A: We do offer wholesale discounts. Prices are entirely dependent on design and size of order. Contact us here and we'll quote you.

Q:What is the cost?

A: Prices vary based on designs and quantity. Give us a call or email us here to get an SRP (Suggested Retail Price).

Q: Why are you in Thailand and the United States?

A: Our factory and headquarters are in Bangkok, Thailand. We also have a sole distributer, Best in Class Impex LLC, in the United States.

Q: Do you guys ship worldwide?

A: Yes, we do ship worldwide. There is a 150 pieces minimum order for shipping. This can be a mix of any designs.

Q: How are you environmentally sustainable?

A: We recently brought in environmental consultants to our factory. Over a year long process, we overhauled our processes, equipment and retrained our staff to hit key metrics including 35% less carbon emission and 50% reduction in water consumption among other environmental goals that fit with our culture and model.
